The video project was a collaborative project with different technological skill uses like group work, camera use, and editing skills. My group consisted of Drew Hartman and Kaelyn Samaha. We started the project with story boards which was like the "sketches" of what we wanted our video to look like. During filming we had to adjust our script because of different ideas as well as the time requirements.

At the end of the video we decided to add bloopers to where the credits are appearing onto the screen to keep the audience engaged.

This project's purpose is to investigate a new form of communication technology using Adobe InDesign. We as a class had various different options that we could choose like a compact disc, walkie-talkie, radio, ink jet printer, telegraph, computer mouse/keyboard, etc. I chose the Polaroid Camera.

I started with a basic background that wouldn't take away from what was trying to be said. Then I decided to make the camera the main focus of the graphic, as well as putting the different facts surrounding. I found information from various sources to supply the period in time, explanation of how it fits the Communication Technology Systems Model, operating principle, applications/uses, impacts, and future projections. Looking to the left is the final project that actually won 1st place for my class!
